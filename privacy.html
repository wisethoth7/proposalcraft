Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

  • Proposal content — when you share a proposal via link, the content is stored in our database to enable sharing and e-signature.
  • View events — when a proposal link is opened, we record an anonymous view count (no IP address or identity stored).
  • Signature data — if a client signs, we store the signed status and timestamp.

What we do NOT collect

  • Your Anthropic API key (stored only in your browser)
  • Your name or email (unless you contact support)
  • Visitor IP addresses or tracking cookies
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Gumroad)

Third parties

  • Gumroad — handles payment processing. See gumroad.com/privacy.
  • Anthropic — your API key is used to call Anthropic's API. Your browser communicates directly with Anthropic; we never see your key or prompts.
  • Supabase — proposal data is stored in a Supabase (PostgreSQL) database hosted in the US.

Data retention

Proposal data is retained for 90 days from creation, then automatically deleted. You can request deletion at any time by contacting support.

Your rights

You may request access to or deletion of any data we hold about your proposals. Contact thothwisdom7@gmail.com.
